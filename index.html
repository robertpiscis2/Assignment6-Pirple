My Favorite Food is "Cebiche"

Ceviche, also cebiche, seviche, or sebiche, is a peruvian seafood dish, typically made from fresh raw fish cured in fresh citrus juices, most commonly lemon or lime, and spiced with ají, chili peppers or other seasonings including chopped onions, salt, and coriander. I like a lot this dish because I'm peruvian and I like eating seafood.

How prepares

Ingredients

  • 1 kg of corvina fillet (or a white fish, without fibers, very fresh and consistent)
  • 3 medium onions, feather cut, slightly thick
  • 8 or 10 lemons
  • 5 chili limo chopped small
  • Pepper (optional)
  • Salt
  • 1 ½ tablespoon chopped coriander (not coriander)
  • 3 to 5 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 3 lettuce leaves
  • 3 yellow sweet potatoes
  • 8 corn slices (1 inch)
  • 1 slime chili pepper cut into thin slices for decoration

Preparation

  1. Cook the corn 🌽 in 2 cm slices with some anise grains.
  2. Cook the sweet potatoes 🥔, peel them and cut them 🔪 into 2 cm slices.
  3. Wash the fish 🐟 with water and salt 🧂. Then cut it 🔪 into squares of 1 to 2cm.
  4. Cut the onion 🔪🧅 to the pen, add salt 🧂.
  5. Put the fish 🐟 in a bowl and season with crushed garlic 🧄 and salt 🧂. Add the small chopped limo chili and the freshly squeezed lemon 🍋, the pepper 🌶 and the chopped coriander. Let it rest for 10 minutes⌚. Try the spicy juice 🌶. Put onion on top of the fish 🧅🐟.
  6. Garnish the platter with the corn 🌽, the chili limo in small slices, the sweet potato 🥔 and the lettuce leaves.
